About VGU Online

VIVEKANAND GLOBAL UNIVERSITY ONLINE


Vivekananda Global University (VGU), located in Jaipur, India, was established in 2012. The university offers quality online education and provides Bachelor and Master Online Degree Courses in diverse fields. These programs include business education, Computer Applications, IT, programming, science, Data, and Arts. It is a UGC approved university and recognized by AICTE, NAAC A+, and AIU, ensuring global acceptance.

VGU Online features a dedicated LMS, experienced faculty, and strong job placement support. With a robust platform for online education, VGU ensures that students receive knowledge and develop critical thinking and practical skills that are vital in today's job market. The 2024 Online admissions are open for all master’s and bachelor’s online degree courses in india.

Frequently Asked Question

VGU offers a range of online courses in fields such as engineering, management, law, and architecture. Its popular courses include online MBA, MCA, MA, BBA, BCA, and BA.

Students can apply to VGU online programs through the university's official website. The application process typically involves filling out an online form, submitting required documents, and paying an application fee. link.

Eligibility for enrolling in an online program at VGU varies depending on the course. Generally, applicants should have a 10+2 for online UG programs and an undergraduate degree for online PG courses.

VGU online courses are delivered through a well-facilitated digital platform and an LMS. The platform includes video lectures, an E-library, AI-based learning, E-portfolio development, AI-powered personalizations, and discussions with faculty. It also provides access to digital resources and assignments.

Online VGU offers dedicated support services for students, including academic advising, technical support, and access to the university digital library. Students can also participate in virtual career counselling and alumni networking events.
